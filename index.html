Frequently Asked Questions

Do you really not use credit scores for approval?
We perform a soft inquiry for identity and fraud prevention, but approval decisions are primarily based on your verified income, employment stability, and debt-to-income ratio. Past credit problems are reviewed in context.
How fast can I get approved?
Many applications receive a preliminary decision within 24-48 hours once all income documentation is received. Funding timelines vary by loan type.
Can I really get a mortgage or car loan after foreclosure or repossession?
Yes. We have helped thousands of clients in exactly that situation. We focus on your current ability to repay, not past events that are behind you.
What states do you serve?
We provide financing solutions in all 50 states and Washington, D.C. through our nationwide lending platform and partner bank in Florida.
Is there a fee to apply?
No application or upfront fees. We are transparent about all costs. You only pay standard closing costs if your loan is approved and funded.
